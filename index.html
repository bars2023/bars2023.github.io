Oct 27, 2023 - Stanford University

Bay Area Robotics Symposium

The event will be streamed and recorded.

About

The 2023 Bay Area Robotics Symposium aims to bring together roboticists from the Bay Area. The program will consist of a mix of faculty, student and industry presentations. For more details, see the event schedule.

Faculty Organizers:

Venue

The event will be at David & Joan Traitel Building of Hoover Institution, 435 Lasuen Mall, Stanford, CA 94305.

Ticket is sold out. The event will be livestreamed and recorded.
